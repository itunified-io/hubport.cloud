A — Collapsible Spaces (Tree View)

Spaces as expandable sections with nested channels. Space header shows icon + name + channel count. Channels indent underneath. Lock icon for private rooms (Älteste). Unread badges on individual channels.

B — Space Cards (Flat List)

Spaces as cards. Click a Space card to drill into its channels (new view replaces list). Channel names shown as preview text. Clean and simple — no nesting visible until you tap in.
