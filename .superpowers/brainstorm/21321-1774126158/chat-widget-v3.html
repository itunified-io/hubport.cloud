Chat RBAC Permissions

Permission Scope Who
chat.view Read messages in joined spaces All publishers
chat.send Send messages in spaces where write is granted All publishers
chat.write:space Write in a specific space/channel Per-channel RBAC (shown as badge)
chat.createSpace Create new spaces Coordinator, Secretary
chat.crossTenant Create/join cross-tenant spaces (federation) Coordinator, Secretary (requires CROSS-TENANT badge)
chat.invite Invite publishers from other tenants to cross-tenant spaces Space admin (overseer of the space)
